What is Scaffold?

Scaffold is an online communication platform that connects people seeking to work with people looking to hire through a quick, direct, and dependable process.

Using a dependable review system and a managed messaging features, Scaffold minimizes potential abuse and provides a space for local micro-economies to fourish.

Unlike most of our competitors, we understand that that a temporary job is much more than just a simple transaction. We believe that every party has a right to know what they are getting into beforehand, and make an informed decision about whether they accept a job or not.

Be steering clear of anonymous posts, allowing a two way system that is easily accessible, and allowing parties to communicate before reaching a working agreement; we make sure that both job posters and seekers feel comfortable.

How does this, "managed chat" system work?

We understand that clear communication before a transaction is important to all parties. But we also understand that most people don't want to put there private details on the internet for all the world to see. Which is why we've built in our own personal chat system.

We also seek to minimize the potential for harassment or unwanted messages, which is why you can only communicate with a user who you have an open application with. That means, as an employer, you can communicate with any applicants as soon as they apply and immediately cease communication by rejecting their application. Likewise as a worker, you're able to send messages to job posters the second you apply and withdraw from communcation at any time.
